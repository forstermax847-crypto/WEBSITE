The story

A corner of Bath, kept warm

Matt was born and raised in Bath, and he's been behind this counter nearly every day for nineteen years — the cafe runs 363 days a year. Some might say workaholic. He'd put it more simply: "I just love food."

The belief hasn't changed: good value, fantastic portions, and proper homemade cooking. Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and a proper plateful is a healthier way to eat than most people give it credit for — especially with our no-or-less-spread option, and nothing swimming in fat.

It has grown into one of the largest cafes in Bath: an award-winning room seating 120, with a wonderful garden for the sunny mornings. And if you can't come to us, we'll come to you — Matty's is the only cafe in Bath that delivers full English breakfasts to your door, free on orders over £12.
